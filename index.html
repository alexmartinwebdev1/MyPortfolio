WatchShop Ecommerce

This is an ecommerce website that I created using NodeJS / Express on the backend and React on the front end. A user can browse the website, look at products, add them to cart and after creating an account they can proceed to checkout where I integrated payments with PayPal. There is also the posibility to be an admin, and then you can add products, mark orders as delivered and make other users admins. You can also update your profile and you can also leave reviews, but no more the one review per product for every user.
